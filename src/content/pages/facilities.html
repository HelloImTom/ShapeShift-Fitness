FACILITIES

Our Main Gym

Top-of-the-Line Gym Equipment

Welcome to our main gym, the heart of Shapeshift Fitness. Experience a state-of-the-art facility equipped with modern fitness equipment, weights, and personalized workout areas. Achieve your fitness goals in a dynamic and motivating environment.

The Studio

Dynamic Fitness Classes & More

Discover 'The Studio, where we host energizing group fitness classes and wellness sessions. Our experienced trainers lead everything from high-intensity workouts to serene yoga sessions. Explore a diverse range of classes to suit your fitness preferences and goals.

The Aquafit Pool

Dive into Aquatic Wellness

Dive into aquatic wellness in our 25-meter pool. Enjoy low-impact exercises, water volleyball, and aquatic therapy sessions. Our spacious pool promotes strength, flexibility, and recovery in a soothing aquatic setting.

  • Sauna and Steam Rooms
